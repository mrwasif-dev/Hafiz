Who we are

Serving Students, Scholars & Institutions

Al-Hafiz Islamic Research Institute was founded by Hafiz Mahmood to give students, madaris, and professionals a dependable partner for research, writing, document conversion, and design — carried out with honesty and academic accuracy.

Our story

Built on Trust & Standards

What began as help for a handful of students composing their theses has grown into a complete research and design workshop — supporting Tanzeem-ul-Madaris, Kanz-ul-Madaris, MPhil, MA and other academic bodies, as well as everyday professionals who need clean, reliable documents.

Every project — whether it's a full mقالہ, a single CV, or a YouTube thumbnail — passes through the same standard: accurate, proofread, on time, and priced fairly.
